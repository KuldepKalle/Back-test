Strategy
VWAP + EMA9 Rules: Signal when 1H candle closes above (BUY) or below (SELL) both VWAP and EMA9 for the first time. 4H candle in same direction = high-confidence trade.
Trailing SL (max 1:3): Entry → SL at −$50 | Hit 1:1 → SL to Break Even | Hit 1:2 → SL locks $50 | Hit 1:3 → Exit $150 profit ✓
Fibonacci 0.618 Retracement Rules — pure price action, no VWAP/EMA9:
📈 BUY SETUP
1. 4H candles: bullish (green, higher highs)
2. 1H candles: bullish (green, uptrend)
3. Identify Swing LOW → Swing HIGH (up-move)
4. Price pulls back to 0.618 level (golden ratio)
5. BUY entry at 0.618 · SL below Swing LOW
6. TP at 1:3 with trailing SL
📉 SELL SETUP
1. 4H candles: bearish (red, lower lows)
2. 1H candles: bearish (red, downtrend)
3. Identify Swing HIGH → Swing LOW (down-move)
4. Price rallies back to 0.618 level (golden ratio)
5. SELL entry at 0.618 · SL above Swing HIGH
6. TP at 1:3 with trailing SL
